How Impressions Work

1 impression = 1 time your ad is displayed to a visitor. If a visitor sees your banner 3 times, that's 3 impressions. Our pricing is based on total impressions served, not unique visitors.

  • Starter ($49): 10,000 impressions = roughly 2,000-5,000 unique visitors see your ad
  • Growth ($149): 50,000 impressions = roughly 10,000-25,000 unique visitors see your ad
  • Premium ($349): Unlimited = every visitor sees your ad, every time they visit

When your impressions run out, your ad pauses until the next month. With Growth and Premium, you can also buy additional impression packs.
