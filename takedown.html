DMCA Policy

EggerMath respects the intellectual property rights of others. If you believe that your copyrighted work has been copied in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please submit a takedown request using the form below.

What to Include

Your takedown request must include:

  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  • The URL of the game or page on EggerMath that contains the infringing material
  • Your full name, email address, and physical address
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Submit a Request

Fill out the form below and we will review your request within 48 hours.

Counter-Notification

If your content was removed and you believe it was a mistake, you may submit a counter-notification. Include your name, the removed content URL, and a statement under penalty of perjury that the removal was a mistake.
